About the Awesome Doctor

Dr. Michael Flicker is a general and advanced interventional gastroenterologist dedicated to compassionate and comprehensive patient care. After graduating from the University of Pennsylvania, Dr. Flicker returned to his hometown of Chicago to attend medical school at the University of Chicago and received his Medical Doctorate in 2004. Dr. Flicker continued his medical training at the University of Chicago, completing his Internal Medicine residency. He trained in Gastroenterology at Harvard's Brigham and Women's Hospital and took classes at the Harvard School of Public Health. Dr. Flicker pursued an additional year of advanced interventional endoscopic training at Northwestern Memorial Hospital from 2011-2012 where he also served on faculty as an Instructor of Medicine.
